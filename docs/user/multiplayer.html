Multiplayer

After pressing the button "Multiplayer" you`ll get in the Multiplayer lobby.

If your PC have Internet connection, you`ll see list of available Internet games on the left side of lobby.
You can join any game you see in the list, or you can join specific game by clicking "Join IP" button. Here you can also create your own server by pressing Host button.

After creation or connect to other server you`ll get in game setup screen.
It looks similar to the one you`ve seen playing scirmish games, but there are three main differences:

- you can turn on/off "advertise on internet" button, it allows other gamers to see your server in global internet servers list in the Multiplyaer lobby.

- a chat interface is available to chat with other players in the game

- when you are ready to play - click on the button to the right from your nick.

You can allow/forbid using of different units (in case you are the host) by pressing "units" button.
Also - you can change maximum number of units per player and some other stuff.


To play Multiplayer games you need port 4242 to be open!
